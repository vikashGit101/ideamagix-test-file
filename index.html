Eligibility For Treatment.

Any individual who has tried diet and exercise but failed to loose weight is considerd as a good candidate for gastric bypass. Although gastric bypass aids in weight loss, it is not an appropriate option of everyone. Based on the overall health and certain medical guidelines, the doctor will decide whether you are eligible for gastric bypass or not.

In genral, gastric bypass could be a good option if:

  • The BMI(body mass index) is 40 or more
  • Your BMI is 30 or more with severe weight related health Conditions like type-2 diabetes and high blood pressure
  • You are willing to make permanent changes in lifestyle
